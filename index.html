Cool Hunting Design Tangents

Podcast Identity, 2023

Designed and developed the identity for Cool Hunting's Design Tangents Podcast, which is hosted by Evan Orensten and Josh Rubin. The podcast takes listeners on a captivating journey into the world of creative processes and inspirations across various industries, showcasing the talents of artists, designers, tech pioneers, and business leaders.

Listen on Apple Podcast, Spotify.

The podcast art is created in Cinema4D, each plane serves as a light source, producing a mesmerizing blend of translucence and dynamic shadows that add depth to the artwork.

New York, New York
